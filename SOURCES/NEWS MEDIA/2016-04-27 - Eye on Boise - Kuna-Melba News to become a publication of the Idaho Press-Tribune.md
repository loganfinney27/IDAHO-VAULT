Here’s a news item from the [[Associated Press]]: 
KUNA, Idaho (AP) — A weekly newspaper in the [[Treasure Valley|southwest Idaho]] town of [[Kuna]] will become a publication of the [[Nampa]]-based [[Idaho Press|Idaho Press-Tribune]]. The Idaho Press-Tribune reports that its parent company, Pioneer News Group, is in the process of buying the [[Kuna Melba News]] from Bellevue, [[Washington]]-based RIM Publications in a deal expected to be finalized this week. Idaho Press-Tribune President and Publisher [[Matt Davison]] says the addition of the newspaper offers enormous potential to provide top-notch local journalism to [[Kuna]] and [[Melba]] for many years to come. Idaho Press-Tribune officials say that starting next week the [[Kuna Melba News]] will have a new design, a larger broadsheet page format and more coverage of city government, schools, business and the community.
