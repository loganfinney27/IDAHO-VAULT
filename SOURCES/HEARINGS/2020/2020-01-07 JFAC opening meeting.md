**Revenue Forecast**

Budget was set expecting 7% growth.

Private business & economists contributed to forecasting.
- extend [[revenue forecast]] from 3 years to 5
- alternative forecasts (optimistic, pessimistic)
- Assigned probabilities to each scenario

Baseline forecast (55% prob) – year to year growth, up to $4,756 million by FY 2024

Pessimistic (35% prob) – $4,579 million by FY 2024

The gap between these scenarios increases year to year

Governor’s budget uses weighted average, rather than relying on one inaccurate forecast

Even with pessimistic scenario, can leave $40 million ending balance

FY 2021 is first time in state history with over $4 *billion* budget
