[[RS]] hearings 

3116 - Medical Liens - [[John Gannon]] 
- "to give a little bit more time to the [[hospitals|hospital]] to file their lien" 
- if no third party payer: 90 days after discharge or the last day services are provided 
- if third party: 90 days after discharge or 30 days after adjustment by insurance carrier 
- Dem substitute moves to introduce, unanimous [[voice vote]] 

31080C1 - Self Storage Facilities - [[John Vander Woude]] 
- lobbyist [[Tim Olson]] representing national self storage group 
- clarify contract relationship between lessee and lessor/owners 
- several drafting edits from industry to be included in [[RS]] motion 
- [[Brent Crane]] says they can print it in [[House State Affairs]] later
- RS returned to Olson to get the edits ironed out before printing 

31046 - Unclaimed Ashes - [[Stephanie Mickelsen]] 
- Some crematoriums in her area have had ashes up to 30 years
- "For veterans, there were ways for them to dispose of those ashes, but not just the general public."
- [[Counties]] are responsible for indigent burial but there is no way for the facility to dispose of the remains under current law. 

31148 - Life Insurance - [[Kathy Peterson]] from [[Meridian]] 
- mother passed in 2012, had paid 30 years on life insurance, didn't receive warning letters via first class mail, involuntary forfeiture 
- would add 'certified mail with signed return receipt' to statute as another option in addition to first class mail 
- [[Brent Crane]] moves to introduce, unanimous voice vote

31186 - Electricians, Public Utilities, Meters - [[Joe Palmer]] 
- utilities are forcing policy change to require meters for each individual tenant in a commercial building 
- [[Brandon Mitchell]] moves to introduce, unanimous voice vote

31048C1 - DOPL Licensure Renewal - [[Jeff Ehlers]] 
- 46-page bill that broadly touches all the licensing boards 
- changes license renewal periods from 1 year to 2 years
- changes expiration date of license to holder's birthdate 
- [[Brent Crane]] moves to introduce, 

31214 - short term rentals - [[Jordan Redman]] 
- allows local governments to regulate short-term rentals in the interest of public health and safety
- calls for safety disclosure by property owner
- allows local jurisdictions to license short term rentals
