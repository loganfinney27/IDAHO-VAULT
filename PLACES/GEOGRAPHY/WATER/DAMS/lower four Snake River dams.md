four federally-controlled [[dams]] on the [[Snake River]] in [[Washington]]; built and operated by [[U.S. Army Corps of Engineers]] with power sold by [[Bonneville Power Administration]] 
- [[Ice Harbor dam]] - [[Lake Sacajawea]]
- [[Lower Monumental dam]] - [[Lake Herbert G. West]] 
- [[Little Goose dam]] - [[Lake Bryan]] 
- [[Lower Granite dam]] - [[Lower Granite Lake]]
