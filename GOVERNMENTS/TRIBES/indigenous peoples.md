Inhabitants of [[North America]] before [[Europe|European]] contact 

The [[State of Idaho]] is home to five [[tribes]] federally recognized by the [[United States of America]]. 
- [[Kootenai Tribe]] - [[Kootenai Indian Reservation]]
- [[Coeur d'Alene Tribe]] - [[Coeur d'Alene Indian Reservation]]
- [[Nez Perce Tribe]] - [[Nez Perce Indian Reservation]]
- [[Shoshone-Bannock Tribes]] - [[Fort Hall Indian Reservation]]
- [[Shoshone-Paiute Tribes]] - [[Duck Valley Indian Reservation]]
