The Idaho Criminal Justice Commission (ICJC) was created by a 2005 [[Governor]]’s [[executive order]]. Made up of 27 members from three branches of government and the community, the ICJC and its subcommittees address important criminal justice issues and challenges, and develop and propose balanced, cost-effective, best-practice solutions to achieve a safer Idaho. The commission meets approximately ten times per year.
