*The Idaho Broadband Advisory Board was created by the Idaho Legislature in 2021, via the passage of [[(2021) House Bill 127|House Bill 127]]. The board consists of three members from the [[Idaho House of Representatives]], three from the [[Idaho Senate]] and three appointed by the [[Governor]].
The board established the Idaho [[Broadband Strategic Plan.pdf|Broadband Strategic Plan]], which supports the board’s vision that Idahoans have access to affordable and reliable [[broadband]] [[infrastructure]]. The board is responsible for structuring, prioritizing and dispersing state and federal grants, which will enhance connectivity across the state and address equal access to economic development, [[remote work|telework]], public safety, [[telehealth]] and [[education]].*
