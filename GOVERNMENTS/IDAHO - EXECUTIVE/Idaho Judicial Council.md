>The Idaho Judicial Council was created by the [[Idaho Legislature|legislature]] in 1967 to investigate and act on allegations of a [[judge]]’s misconduct or disability. It is composed of three citizen members, two attorneys, one [[district judges|district jude]] and the [[Chief Justice]] of the [[Idaho Supreme Court]], who review and act on all complaints made against judges.

Provides names for selection by the [[Governor]] to fill [[judges|judicial]] vacancies.
