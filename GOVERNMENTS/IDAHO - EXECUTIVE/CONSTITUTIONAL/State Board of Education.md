Retains general supervision of K-12 education under the [[Idaho Constitution]]. 

Establishes general policy for [[school districts]] and [[charter schools]].

Seven members appointed by the [[Governor]] plus the [[Superintendent of Public Instruction]].

Board of Regents for [[University of Idaho|UIdaho]] and Board of Trustees for [[Boise State University|BSU]], [[Idaho State University|ISU]] and [[PLACES/SCHOOLS/COLLEGES AND UNIVERSITIES/STATE COLLEGES/Lewis-Clark State College|LCSC]].
