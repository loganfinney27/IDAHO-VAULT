[[U.S. Department of Defense]] youth outreach [[STEM]] program 

https://www.imd.idaho.gov/starbase/

_DoD STARBASE Idaho began serving Idaho’s students in the summer of 2018. Since then the program has grown to provide STEM enrichment for 7 districts, inspiring approximately 2,000 students a year. DoD STARBASE Idaho’s three classrooms operate 5 days a week during the traditional school year, as well as providing out-of-school camps both on and off [[Gowen Field]] throughout the year. 
In the 2020/2021 School Year STARBASE Idaho began its 2.0 afterschool program utilizing the First Lego Robotics Challenge at Middleton Middle School. In the 2021/2022 school year, the 2.0 program expanded to serve 6th graders at Grace Jordan and Whitney Elementary Schools in Boise._
