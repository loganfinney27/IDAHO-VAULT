*Bicameral legislative branch of the [[State of Idaho]]. Administrative management by [[Legislative Council]] and the [[Legislative Services Office]].*
- **lower chamber**: [[Idaho House of Representatives]] - [[House member|Representatives]] 
- **upper chamber**: [[Idaho Senate]] - [[Senate member|Senators]] 
- citizen committees: [[Committee on Legislative Compensation]], [[Idaho Commission for Redistricting]]
- standing joint committees: [[Legislative Council]], [[Change in Employee Compensation Committee]], [[Economic Outlook and Revenue Assessment Committee]], [[Joint Finance-Appropriations Committee]], [[Joint Legislative Oversight Committee]], [[Joint Millennium Fund Committee]] 
- active interim committees: [[Committee on Federalism]], [[Council on Indian Affairs]], [[Child Protection Legislative Oversight Committee]], [[Committee on Uniform Accounting and Transparency]], [[Natural Resources Interim Committee]], [[Occupational and Professional Licensure Review Committee]], [[Medicaid Managed Care Task Force]]
- past interim committees: [[Criminal Justice Reinvestment Oversight Committee]], [[Expenditure of ARPA Funds Study Committee]], [[Property Taxes and Revenue Expenditures Study Committee]], [[Idaho Content Standards Study Committee]], [[Intrastate Commercial Air Service Study Committee]], [[Occupational Licensing and Certification Laws Committee]], [[Capitol Services Committee]], [[Equitable Assessment of Costs Related to Medicaid Expansion Committee]]
- past working groups: [[working group - Funding Construction of Public Schools]], [[working group - Judicial Council]], [[working group - K-12 Health Insurance]], [[working group - Education]], [[working group - Finance-Appropriations]], [[working group - State Affairs]], [[working group - Judiciary and Rules]], [[working group - Property Tax]]
