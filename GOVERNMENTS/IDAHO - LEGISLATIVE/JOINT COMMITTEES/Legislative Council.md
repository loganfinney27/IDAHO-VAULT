The Legislative Council oversees the management responsibilities of the [[Idaho Legislature]]. 
**Membership:**
- [[Senate President Pro Tempore|President Pro Tempore]] 
- [[Speaker of the House]]
- [[House Majority Leader]] 
- [[Senate Majority Leader]]
- [[House Minority Leader]] 
- [[Senate Minority Leader]]
- four [[Idaho Senate|Senate]] members, two from each party
- four [[Idaho House of Representatives|House]] members, two from each party
