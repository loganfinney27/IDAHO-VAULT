>*The [[United States of America|American]] Redoubt is a political migration movement first proposed in 2011 by survivalist novelist and [[Internet|blogger]] [[James Wesley Rawles]] which designates [[State of Idaho|Idaho]], [[Montana]], and [[Wyoming]] along with eastern parts of [[Oregon]] and [[Washington]], as a safe haven for conservative [[Christianity|Christians]].* 
>-Wikipedia

>*a loosely defined group of preppers, [[U.S. Libertarian Party|libertarians]], and conservative [[Christianity|Christians]] who have moved to the thinly populated areas of [[Montana]], [[State of Idaho|Idaho]], [[Wyoming]], [[Oregon]], and [[Washington]].*
>-Anne Helen Petersen
